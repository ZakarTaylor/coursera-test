Simple Waffle Recipe

    Dry Ingredients
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. 1 teaspoon salt
  3. 4 teaspoons baking powder
  4. 2 tablespoons white sugar
    Wet Ingredients (in a separate bowl)
  1. 2 large eggs
  2. 1 ½ cups warm milk
  3. 115g Buttermilk
  4. ⅓ cup butter, melted
  5. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    Instructions
  1. Combine the wet ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Pour the wet mixture into the bowl of dry ingredients and mix well.
  3. Scoop 1/2 cup of batter into a large waffle maker.
  4. Enjoy!
